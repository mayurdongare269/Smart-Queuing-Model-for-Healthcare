About Us

Welcome to Smart OPD Queuing Model, where innovation meets healthcare to transform patient experiences. Our mission is simple yet profound: to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers by reducing waiting times and simplifying the journey to quality care.

We understand the importance of time, especially in medical scenarios, and we’re dedicated to providing a seamless, efficient experience for everyone involved. Our platform enables patients to book appointments, track their position in real time, and receive timely notifications—making healthcare visits smoother and stress-free.

At Smart Queuing Model, we’re not just about queue management; we’re about empowering patients and healthcare providers alike. By offering personalized profiles, multi-language support, and virtual consultation options, we aim to make healthcare interactions as accessible and compassionate as possible.
